Kickball Rules

Welcome to the Levittown Kickball rules page! Please read through the following rules to ensure fair play and fun for all participants.

General Rules

  • Each team must have at least 8 players on the field.
  • The game is played with a standard kickball.
  • Each game consists of 7 innings.
  • A team must score at least 5 runs to win the game.

Field Rules

  • The field is divided into bases: home, first, second, and third.
  • Players must touch each base in order to score a run.
  • Outs can be made by catching a kicked ball before it touches the ground or tagging a runner with the ball.

Player Conduct

  • No aggressive behavior or unsportsmanlike conduct will be tolerated.
  • Players must respect the umpire's decisions.
  • Any player ejected from a game will be suspended for the next game.

Please contact us if you have any questions about the rules or need clarification!
Do not hestitate to ask us at the field as well!
