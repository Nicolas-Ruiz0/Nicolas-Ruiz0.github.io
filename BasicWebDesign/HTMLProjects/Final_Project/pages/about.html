About Levittown Kickball

Levittown Kickball is a community held event in the summer for people to enjoy the outdoors and the enjoyable game of kickball.


Being a relatively big community, we usually produce around 4-6 teams with 10-12 people per team. Our goal is to get people active and to have a friendly and fun competition.
We want to bring people together, no matter who you are (you must be 15 or older to play), we want you to come out and have fun!

How It Works

  • Registration opens in early June, and it is open for 2 weeks
  • People Register through either the website or they reenter from the last season
  • Then team captains are chosen randomly(can't have more than one captain from the same family), and then they are to choose the players they want
  • After the teams are chosen, we will have a bracket reveal to show who plays who.
  • And then the games start.
  • Important Note: Choosing captains, teams, and playing the matches are all on the same day. But if there happens to be too many teams then the games will be stretched out to more than one day
