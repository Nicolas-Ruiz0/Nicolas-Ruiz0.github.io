How To Embed a Service-Hosted Video Into Your Website

By Nicolas Ruiz

Step 0.5:

Head on over to a Service-Hosted Video Website
For example: YouTube

Step 1:

Click on the video you want to embed

Step 2:

Click on the Share button

Step 3:

Click on the Embed button and Click Copy

Step 4:

Paste the code into your HTML file


And then your done!
