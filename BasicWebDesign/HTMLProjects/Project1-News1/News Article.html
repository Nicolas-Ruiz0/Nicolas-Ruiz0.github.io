Grand Opening of Curio & Co.!

Discover the Unexpected!

Information

Spanning from Artisan goods, gifts and novelties, home decor, books, and playsets and toys there is everything for people of all ages. Curio & Co. want to engage with the community and supply people with activities ranging from book clubs and learning craftsmanship included within a Loyalty Program. For any local Artisans, Curio & Co. are willing to purchase creations you make and put them on display, with negotiable prices. Curio & Co. are all about being eco-friendly, with special events anyone can volunteer for, at almost no cost.

Loyalty Program

The Loyalty Program within Curio & Co. is designed to reward loyal customers and entice newer customers to joining the programs with the exclusive benefits and activities/clubs you can enter. For every dollar spent you can get one point, with enough accumulated points you can redeem discounts on future purchases. Curio & Co. love to celebrate birthdays and holidays, when you birthday arrives you get special discounts or even a special gift. When you are in the Loyalty Program you can enter clubs and volunteer for special events(can vary from being exclusive for members and not being a member), such as Book Club for book lovers, Craftsmanship Club where you can learn to make accessories and home decor.

Final Thoughts

Curio & Co. is a brand new experience within a neighborhood with unique ways of interacting with the community while still providing. With a unique selection of goods you can purchase for good deals. Keeping customers waiting and involved with a good engagement planned for the store. A cheap, high value membership, allowing customers to be loyal and be rewarded with many different ways.
But Overall, Curio & Co. is a commmunity hub where people can connect with each other, with a unique prodect selection with options to get custom made prodcut designed for you!

Our mission at The Curious Corner is to spark creativity and connection in every visit. We believe that every item we offer has a unique story, waiting to be discovered.
